## 🎨 Brand Identity System

### Colors Extracted from Logo
- **Primary**: Forest Green `#1B4D3E` - Growth, trust, Africa
- **Secondary**: Emerald `#2D6A4F` - Vibrant, fresh
- **Accent**: Gold `#C9A227` - Premium, wealth, aspiration
- **Neutral**: Sand `#D4C5B0` - Warm, earthy
- **Background**: Cream `#FAF9F6` - Clean, sophisticated

### Typography
- **Display**: Playfair Display - Luxury, editorial
- **Body**: Inter - Modern, highly readable
- **Scale**: 6-level type system from 12px to 72px

### Design Language
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent cards with backdrop blur
- **Animations**: Smooth Framer Motion transitions
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first, breakpoints at 640px, 768px, 1024px, 1280px

## 📈 Go-To-Market Strategy

### Target Audiences
1. **Diaspora Professional** (Primary) - 28-45, UK/US/UAE/Canada, $75K-$250K income
2. **Local High-Earner** - 32-50, Nigeria, ₦15M-₦100M income
3. **Global Investor** - 35-55, international, seeking African exposure
4. **First-Time Builder** - 25-35, young professionals, entry-level investments

### Content Strategy
- **LinkedIn**: B2B focus, thought leadership, market insights
- **Instagram**: Visual storytelling, property showcases, lifestyle
- **YouTube**: Educational content, podcast, development diaries
- **Newsletter**: Weekly "The Gateway" - market pulse, exclusive opportunities
- **Podcast**: "The VELD Sessions" - weekly investor interviews

### Content Calendar
- Monday: Newsletter send
- Tuesday: LinkedIn + Instagram
- Wednesday: YouTube/Podcast drop
- Thursday: Client stories
- Friday-Sunday: Engagement content
